Our History & Founder

Jimmy "Jim" Dollar, Sr. founded Dollar Concrete Construction Company, Inc. in the mid-1970's on the basic principle of providing quality concrete work at a fair price.

Over the past 40 years, Dollar has expanded both their client base and their market reach, and continues to provide superior concrete construction services for our clients around the southeastern United States. Many of Dollar's current clients have been so for in excess of 25 years.

Dollar currently operates in eight southeastern states (Georgia, Tennessee, Kentucky, Alabama, Mississippi, North Carolina, South Carolina, and Virginia), and performs "turn-key" services for our clients in all phases of horizontal and vertical concrete and masonry construction.

Our Reach

Dollar's reach covers the vast majority of the southeastern United States. We perform concrete turn-key concrete work in Georgia, Tennessee, Kentucky, Alabama, Mississippi, North Carolina, South Carolina, and Virginia.

Our turn-key masonry division, "DC Masonry" covers Georgia, Kentucky, Alabama, Mississippi, North Carolina, South Carolina, and Virginia.
