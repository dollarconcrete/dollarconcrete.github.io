Multi-Family

Dollar has been providing turn-key structural and site-related concrete work for apartment buildings and condominiums throughout the southeast for over 40 years. Projects have ranged from multi-structure "garden-style" complexes, to urban "wrap" projects surrounding multi-story parking structures.

Commercial

Commercial construction is also a significant part of Dollar's yearly production. From retail stores, to fueling stations, offices, and distribution warehouses, Dollar's staff and crews meet the needs and schedules for our retail clients.

Senior Living

In the past decade, the need for senior living / care facilities has continued to expand in all of the markets Dollar serves around Georgia and the Southeast. With the completion of nearly two-dozen senior-living / care facilities this past decade alone, Dollar has proven experience in this growing and important market.

Military & Federal

Dollar Concrete has constructed numerous projects around the Southeast for the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers and the Federal Bureau of Prisons, including military barracks, hardstand tank trails, training & maintenance facilities, and federal prisons.

Student Housing

Student housing facilities (dormitories as well as apartments) are similar to other multi-family projects, but many are complicated by compressed time frames in which to perform the work. Dollar's record of jobsite communication and coordination with our clients has proven to be successful.

Distribution & Warehouse

Dollar has constructed a number of large foundation & slab projects in and around the southeast for regional distribution centers as well as both industrial & commercial warehouses.
