About DC Masonry

Welcome to DC Masonry, LLC. As a sister-company to Dollar Concrete Construction, DC Masonry provides full-service, "turnkey" masonry services throughout Georgia and around the Southeast. With our 25+ years of masonry knowledge and experience, the DC Masonry team can successfully provide the DIV 04 construction needs for your next project.

DC Masonry, LLC is also headquartered at Dollar's corporate campus in Norcross, GA. Please feel free to explore our page and familiarize yourself with our services and some of our projects.

Services

Our experienced staff of estimators, project managers & superintendents tailor our services both in the office and field to meet the various needs of the client, while adapting to the unique and specific requirements of each project. DC Masonry, LLC performs work in the southeastern states of Georgia, Alabama, North Carolina & South Carolina.

Block (CMU)

From structural concrete masonry units to architectural & specialty block, we provide complete, turn-key installations of CMU for all types of multi-family, commercial, senior living, and student housing projects.

Brick Veneer

Brick veneer is still one of the most sustainable & visually attractive building elements used in the industry. Architectural patterning, sizes & color blends have continued to become more popular. Our staff can provide full-service estimating & installations, as well as cost-saving alternates.
