About Us

Dollar Concrete Construction Company is a privately-owned commercial concrete building contractor, servicing a wide variety of markets throughout the Southeastern United States. For over 40 years, Dollar Concrete has provided superior quality services to their clients for "turn-key" concrete construction. Our goal has always been to build and maintain long-lasting relationships through providing a quality product at a competitive price. Many of Dollar's clients have been so for more than 25 years.

Dollar currently operates throughout eight southeastern states (Georgia, Tennessee, Kentucky, Alabama, Mississippi, North Carolina, South Carolina, and Virginia) and performs services in all phases of horizontal and vertical concrete construction. The Dollar family of companies also provides turn-key masonry construction (DC Masonry, LLC) , rebar (Premier Rebar, LLC), and forming services (DC Forming).
